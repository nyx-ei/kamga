# Chifor Fundraizer

A platform for managing fundraising operations (levees) within RPN associations. Chifor Fundraizer brings transparency, traceability, and simplicity to the way associations collect and distribute funds.

## The Problem

RPN associations manage levees manually -- often with paper records, spreadsheets, or informal tracking. This leads to:

- Lack of transparency for members on how funds are collected and distributed
- Administrative burden on association leaders
- No audit trail for financial operations
- Difficulty scaling across large member bases

## What Chifor Fundraizer Does

**For Association Members**
- View upcoming and ongoing levees in real time
- Track personal contributions and payment history
- Receive notifications for levee events
- Access transparent reports on fund collection and distribution

**For Association Administrators**
- Create and manage levees end-to-end (initiation, collection, distribution)
- Manage member registrations and profiles
- Monitor contributions with a real-time dashboard
- Generate financial reports and audit trails
- Oversee multiple levees simultaneously

**For the RPN Ecosystem**
- Standardized levee management across associations
- Centralized member directory
- Cross-association visibility for members belonging to multiple groups
